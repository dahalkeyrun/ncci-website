About the Conference

National Conference on Computer Innovations

The National Conference on Computer Innovations (NCCI 2025) is a premier academic event that brings together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals from the field of computing and information technology. The conference aims to provide a platform for sharing knowledge, discussing emerging trends, and fostering collaborations in the rapidly evolving field of computing.

NCCI 2025 will feature keynote speeches by renowned experts, technical paper presentations, workshops, and panel discussions. The conference will be held on August 24, 2025 at Kathmandu University, one of Nepal's leading institutions for higher education and research.

Conference Scope

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems
  • Software Engineering and Architecture
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Embedded Systems
  • Computer Vision and Image Processing
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Quantum Computing
  • Computational Mathematics

About KUCC

The Kathmandu University Computer Club (KUCC) is a student-run organization at Kathmandu University dedicated to promoting computing and technology among students. KUCC organizes various technical events, workshops, and competitions throughout the year to enhance students' technical skills and foster innovation.

About DoCSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DoCSE) at Kathmandu University is a leading center for computer science education and research in Nepal. The department offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs and is actively involved in cutting-edge research in various fields of computing.

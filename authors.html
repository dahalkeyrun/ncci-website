Publication Information

All accepted papers will be published in a special edition of the KUSET Journal (Kathmandu University Journal of Science, Engineering and Technology).

Selected papers may be recommended for publication in Scopus-indexed journals after further review and revision.

Important Dates

Paper Submission Deadline June 15, 2025
Notification of Acceptance July 15, 2025
Camera Ready Submission July 30, 2025
Early Registration Deadline July 15, 2025
Late Registration Deadline August 10, 2025
Conference Date August 24, 2025

Important Notices for Authors

  • At least one author must register for the conference in order for the paper to be accepted and included in the proceedings.
  • All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference.
  • All papers will undergo a double-blind peer review process.
  • Maximum paper length is 8 pages including references.
  • All official communication will be done through the email kucc@ku.edu.np
